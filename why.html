Why Am I Getting This

First and foremost, I think you might find this interesting. In the unlikely event this has blown up and circulation is surpassing my own network–whoever sent it to you thinks you might find this interesting.

Either that, or when I tried to start up a part of this project last year you expressed interest in receiving the updates. Let’s see if I can keep it going this time.

Lastly, hoping to stay in touch and build something more regular and impactful than a string of sporadic in-person and virtual events.

If none of those apply to you, you must be part of the friends and family group receiving this–don’t go too hard on me. I am still the goofball you know and love–hopefully.

Mostly though, all of that to say: I am trying to start a dialogue and inviting you to share your thoughts as well. ∎
